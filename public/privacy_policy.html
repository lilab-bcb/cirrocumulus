Cirrocumulus Privacy Policy

Information gathering

Cirrocumulus receives information related to user's Google profiles, including email addresses and OAuth tokens. This information is gathered as part of the standard Google sign-in process.

Use of information

Cirrocumulus uses the information gathered above to enable integration with Google-based services that require a Google account, such as Google Cloud Storage Platform. At no time do we disclose any user information to outside sources.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy statement, the practices of this site, or your dealings with this site, you can contact us at cumulus@broadinstitute.org.
